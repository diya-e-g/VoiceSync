General Questions

What is Voice Sync, and how does it help people who stutter?
Voice Sync helps people who stutter by allowing them to record their speech, convert it into non-stuttered text, and play back the corrected version to assist with fluency and confidence.
Can I use Voice Sync without an internet connection?
Currently, Voice Sync requires an internet connection to process speech-to-text conversion and utilize chatbot support.

Recording and Playback

How do I record my voice using Voice Sync?
Simply press the microphone button to start recording your speech. Once finished, Voice Sync will convert your speech to text and provide a corrected playback.
Can I edit the transcribed text before playing it back?
No, you cannot manually edit the text before playing back the corrected speech.
What happens if my recording is unclear?
If your recording is unclear, try speaking closer to the microphone in a quiet environment for better accuracy.

Chatbot Assistance

How does the chatbot help with speech improvement?
The chatbot provides emotional support, encourages fluency, and offers word sequencing exercises to improve speech flow.
Can the chatbot provide real-time feedback on my speech?
The chatbot offers feedback based on text input and helps users practice structured speech patterns.
Does the chatbot offer personalized encouragement?
Yes, the chatbot provides personalized encouragement based on user progress and interactions.

Technical Accessibility

Voice Sync works best on modern browsers like Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and Safari, and supports only desktops.
